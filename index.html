Lionel Messi

GREATEST OF ALL TIME

Lionel Andres Messi is an Argentine professional footballer who plays as a forward and captains both Spanish club Barcelona and the Argentina national team. Born and raised in central Argentina, Messi relocated to Spain to join Barcelona at age 13, for whom he made his competitive debut aged 17 in October 2004. Now he is one of the greatest player of all time winnig 6 Ballon-D'Ors
